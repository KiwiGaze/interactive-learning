# Triangles

A triangle is a polygon with three sides and three angles. The sum of interior angles is always 180 degrees.

## Classification by sides

- Scalene — all three sides different
- Isosceles — exactly two sides equal
- Equilateral — all three sides equal (and all angles 60 degrees)

## Classification by angles

- Acute — all three angles less than 90 degrees
- Right — one 90 degree angle
- Obtuse — one angle greater than 90 degrees

## Triangle inequality

For any triangle with sides a, b, c, the sum of any two sides must exceed the third: a + b > c, a + c > b, b + c > a.
